Outside the Window

You look out the window and see only darkness. As suspected, there's definitely something wrong with this bus. Normally, the route home is bustling with cars, but now there is nothing but an endless void. Just as you are about to turn away, a pair of blood-stained hands suddenly grabs onto the window. Startled, you let out a gasp and instinctively cover your mouth but it is too late. Your voice has already drawn the attention of the surrounding spirits...
