Name: Blackpink
Members:lisa, Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé
Blackpink is a South Korean girl group composed of Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa . Launched by YG Entertainment in 2016, the group is known for its unique music style, fashion, and stage presence. Blackpink has garnered massive global fans, with their songs and music videos achieving great success on major music platforms. They are famous for hit singles like "DDU-DU DDU-DU" and "Kill This Love," showcasing strong stage performance and musical prowess. Beyond music, Blackpink has achieved success in fashion and social media, becoming a representative of modern K-pop culture.


Representative Works

Kill This Love

"Kill This Love" is a song by Blackpink, released on April 5, 2019. This pop dance track is known for its intense rhythm and captivating melody.

DDU-DU DDU-DU

"DDU-DU DDU-DU" gained widespread attention and love after its release, with its music video earning billions of views. The song showcases Blackpink's strong vocal talents and stage presence.

How You Like That

"How You Like That" is a song by Blackpink, released on June 26, 2020. This pop dance track is known for its powerful rhythm, stunning arrangement, and confident lyrics.
