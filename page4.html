Inside the Bus

You decide to observe the inside of the bus to prepare for whatever may come. Quietly, you take in the surroundings:

  • There's a faint smell of rot and blood in the air, but you can't pinpoint its source.
  • The bus lighting is a warm yellow, oddly calming.
  • The roof hatch is open.
  • There aren't many passengers, and no one is standing.
  • At every stop, someone gets off and disappears without a trace.
  • Your seat is the closest to the door, making it the easiest for you to get off.

What will you do next?

  • Decide to get off at the next stop
  • Wait until closer to home
